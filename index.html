MochaWolfroo started under a different name in 2014, where the he began uploading fan art to DeviantArt. The person behind the Alias is a small artist named Ben from Michigan. Ben has been drawing since 2010, at the age of 11. He has had inspiration from Disney's Robin Hood, Marvel, DC, as well as Nintendo and PlayStation Franchises. His favourite artist is former Disney director Aaron Blaise. Ben's focus is on animal based characters and illustrations, as well as creatures made based of Mythology and Fantasy.
